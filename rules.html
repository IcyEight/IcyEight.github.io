Checkers Overview

Checkers is a game played on a 8x8 board with each player given 12 pieces of a single color. Traditionally known as a board game, this version of checkers is transformed into an online game that can be played by two players anytime.


Rules

  • Red checkers will always go first.
  • On a single turn, a checker piece may only be moved diagonally one space, with the exception of jumping.
  • A checker piece may be moved more than one space if it is able to jump an opponent's piece. A valid jump is when an opponent's piece is diagonal from the player's piece and that player is able to move his/ her piece over the opponent's piece and onto a blank tile. This movement must also be on a single diagonal.
  • Multiple jumps can be performed on a single turn.

Strategy

  • Always diagonally double up your checkers so that they cannot be jumped.
  • Get your checkers kinged as soon as possible. This will allow them to move freely around the board.
